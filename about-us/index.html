THE ISSUE
Indian farmers continue to face challenges like poor soil health, and limited access to reliable crop guidance. KrishiMitra aims to empower them with accurate predictions, and better decision-making support.
WHAT WE DO
We provide data-driven soil tests, crop predictions and fertilizer recommendations to help smallholders make confident, profitable decisions.
Farmer-first
We design tools and advice around real farmer needs — simple, local and usable on the ground.
Sustainable practices
Promote soil health and climate-smart farming that improves yields while protecting resources.
Data-driven advice
Use soil tests, satellite data and models to give precise, actionable recommendations.
Data Transparency
We use reliable, open government datasets to build transparent, explainable predictions that farmers can trust.
Evidence-Based
Every recommendation—soil, crop, or fertilizer—is powered by verified agricultural research and official datasets.
AI for Agriculture
Machine learning models trained on real farming data help provide accurate insights tailored to each region and season.
Smart Farming
Complex data is converted into simple, actionable steps so farmers can take confident decisions every day.
Continuous learning
We iterate with farmers — learn from every season and improve recommendations over time.
